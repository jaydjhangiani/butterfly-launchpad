# Butterfly Effect — Single-Page Lead Magnet

## Overview

A single-page website for Butterfly Effect coaching that captures leads for a free consultation, matching the pink/blush and teal design of your current Wix site. Leads will be sent to a Google Sheet.

## Design & Branding

- **Color palette**: Soft pink/blush background, teal/mint accents (butterfly motif), dark text
- **Background**: Decorative butterfly line art on the pink background (matching current site)
- **Typography**: Clean, elegant fonts — bold for headings, justified body text
- **Overall feel**: Warm, feminine, inviting — matching your Wix site exactly

## Page Sections

### 1. Hero / Headline Section

- Pink background with decorative butterfly outlines
- Headline: **"Ready to test the waters?"**
- Body text: "1-on-1 coaching with me will help you **find clarity** on **what you want** (in your career and in life), and then get further clear on **how to get there** with a concrete action plan and tools to **stay accountable** to that plan."

### 2. Lead Capture Form (on the same view)

- White card with butterfly illustration on the left
- Persuasive text on the right:
  - "Coaching believes no one size fits all."
  - "We each have goals and plans that are unique to us."
  - "Hence, no one coaching package fits all either."
  - **"Let's have a 10-minute chat to understand what you need and find you the package that will work for you!"**
  - "Need another push into the beautiful waters? **Read the testimonials below.**"
- Form fields:
  - Name
  - Email ID
  - Country Code dropdown + Phone Number
  - "How did you find me?" dropdown
  - Submit button (teal/dark styled)

### 3. Footer

- Contact info, social links, copyright

## Backend: Google Sheets Integration

- A Lovable Cloud edge function receives form submissions and appends rows to your Google Sheet
- You'll provide the Google Sheet ID and we'll configure secure access

## Future-Ready

- Built to expand into a multi-page site with multiple coaching products/services
